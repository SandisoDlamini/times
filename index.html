PUDEMO’S MLUNGISI POISONING: WHY I DID IT - ‘YOUNG BOY’

MBABANE – The man associated with the poisoning of PUDEMO President Mlungisi Makhanya, says he was compelled by personal and financial reasons to take the hard decision.

In a video that circulated on social media yesterday, Sifiso Innocent Mahlangu made startling allegations, giving clues that the contention between him and Makhanya was based on personal circumstances and prompted by apparent lack of financial accountability in the political organisation. He also summarised the situation as a ‘lion mauling its owner’ (libhubesi lidla umnikati walo).He registered discontentment with emaSwati who pretend to be having the best interest of the nation, yet their intention is personal enrichment. “They go to rich countries and ask for money to advance the course of the struggle for change, but after getting the money, nothing goes towards that purpose.

Recording

He said he witnessed this after former Warder Bheka Magagula made a live recording on the scarcity of finances in the camp.
